About The Bistro

The Bistro was set up with an aim of providing delicious food at reasonable prices. Located near the KU School of Business, the Bistro features a casual vibe with a menu reflecting flavors of Assam fused with Continental. We wish we could claim that the menu changes daily, but that really doesn't happen. Right? We can't complain though.
